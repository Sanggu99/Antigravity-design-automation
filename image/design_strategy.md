# Design Strategy: West Seoul Culture Plaza

## Project Overview
- **Name**: West Seoul Culture Plaza
- **Location**: 743 Naebalsan-dong, Gangseo-gu, Seoul (10,017.6 sqm)
- **Concept**: "Culture Cloud" / Organic Fusion
- **Key Programs**: Library, Sports Center (Pool), Kids Cafe

## Design Guidelines & Regulations
- **Zoning**: 2nd Class General Residential
- **Height Limit**: 5 Stories (Airport restrictions apply)
- **Coverage**: 60% (BCR), 200% (FAR)
- **Open Space**: Emphasis on "Open Library" and outdoor connection (Book Garden, Kids Play Area).

## Layout Strategy
- **Configuration**: Three organic masses interconnected by a central lobby/atrium.
- **Orientation**: Main masses oriented South/South-East for sunlight.
- **Zoning**:
  - **North**: Library (Quiet zone, consistent light)
  - **South/East**: Kids Cafe & Sports Center (Active zone, facing park/school)
  - **Central**: Shared community courtyard and entry plaza.
- **Landscape**: Green roofs to blend with the urban context; permeable paving for the plaza.
